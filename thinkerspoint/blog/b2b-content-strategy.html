B2B Content Strategy That Converts

Feb 2025 · Strategy

In B2B and industrial markets, buyers research heavily before contacting sales. Content that answers their questions and demonstrates expertise builds trust and generates leads.

What Works in B2B Content

Technical guides, case studies, comparison content, and thought leadership aligned to the buyer journey. SEO helps your content get found; gated assets and CTAs capture leads.

Building a Content Engine

Map content to stages: awareness, consideration, decision. Use topic clusters and internal linking. Repurpose long-form content into social snippets, emails, and sales collateral.
