Voice AI for Real Estate: Lead Qualification 24/7

Feb 2025 · AI

Property enquiries don't wait for business hours. Voice AI agents with local phone numbers can answer calls, qualify leads, and book site visits around the clock — in Hindi, English, or other languages.

Why Voice AI for Real Estate

Buyers and tenants often call when they see a listing. Missing the call means losing the lead. An AI agent can capture details, answer basic questions, and hand off hot leads to your team.

How It Works

We deploy voice agents with Indian (or other) numbers, trained on your inventory and FAQs. Callers get a natural conversation; you get qualified leads in your CRM. Ideal for developers, brokers, and proptech in India and UAE.
