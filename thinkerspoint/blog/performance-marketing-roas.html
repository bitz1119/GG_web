Performance Marketing: Getting ROAS Right

Mar 2025 · Performance

Return on ad spend (ROAS) is the north star for many performance campaigns. But setting the wrong target or measuring it incorrectly can lead to wasted budget or missed scale.

Setting Realistic ROAS Targets

Base targets on your unit economics: CAC, LTV, and margin. A ROAS that looks good on paper may be unprofitable if conversion quality or lifetime value is low.

Measuring Correctly

Use attribution that reflects your funnel — first-touch, last-touch, or multi-touch depending on the business. Align reporting with your CRM and revenue data.

Scaling Without Burning Budget

Scale channels and audiences gradually. Test creative and landing pages before increasing spend. Our performance marketing team helps brands do this across India, USA, and other markets. Get in touch to learn more.
